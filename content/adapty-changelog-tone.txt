Tone patterns to mirror:
- Lead with the feature name or "What's new" framing. Be direct.
- One sentence per feature: what it does, why it matters, optional doc link.
- Use bullets or short dashes for lists. Keep paragraphs short.
- Include concrete outcomes (e.g., "A couple of clicks and everything is synced", "No more switching tools").
- Docs links as "Read the docs", "Learn more", "Set it up" — action-oriented.
- Keep it practical and clear. Just concrete updates, no hype.
- When multiple features: group by theme, use subheadings or bold feature names.
