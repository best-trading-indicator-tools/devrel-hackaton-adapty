As of January 2026, Apple has started rejecting paywall designs that include a toggle element.

Rejections are being issued under Guideline 3.1.2. In particular, paywalls where a free trial is presented via a toggle (on/off switch) are now considered high risk.

No official announcement has been made, but multiple recent cases suggest that this enforcement is becoming systematic.

If your app allows users to add or remove a trial using a switch-based structure, you may encounter issues in your next update.

At the moment, this appears to affect iOS only. There are no reported changes on the Android side.
