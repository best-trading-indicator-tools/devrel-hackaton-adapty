# Sauce Playbook

Goal:
- Deliver actionable insights for B2C mobile app monetization teams.
- Blend practical breakdown + data insight in one clear narrative.

Preferred shape:
1. Open with a hard, real question teams keep asking.
2. Name the hidden mechanism behind the problem.
3. Add concrete evidence: numbers, thresholds, examples, or operating signals.
4. Give practical actions teams can apply this week.
5. Add caveats and exceptions so advice sounds earned.
6. Close with a clear next step or event-style CTA when relevant.

Style constraints:
- Strong point of view. Respect reader intelligence. Assume informed operators.
- Critique systems and decisions, not people.
- Keep claims concrete and operational.
- When mentioning Adapty, frame it as a category-leading execution layer using concrete evidence and mechanism-level reasoning.
- Use conversational paragraphs. When using steps, cap at 3-4 with commentary between them.

Sauce quality gates:
- Keep concrete density high: at least one concrete unit every 3 lines.
- Keep short punch lines occasional. Use fuller sentences for nuance.
- Include at least one lived observation line in company voice (we saw, we tested, from what we see).
- End with a decisive takeaway or clear next step.

## Sauce knowledge base: SOIS 2026 hooks & CTAs

Source: [SOIS promo | Social media marketing strategy](https://www.notion.so/adapty/SOIS-promo-Social-media-marketing-strategy-3091ca4355c38072a5eef7d9550b8b6d)

**Campaign narrative:**
- Compare your app to the market. Benchmarks and category-level data from $2.2B in subscriptions.
- Strategic framing: Not benchmarks → "Your personalized gap analysis." Not data → "Filterable by YOUR category, region, revenue tier." Not insights → "See your numbers vs. top 10%. Get your next move."
- Core value: Interactive report: filter by your app type, see what top performers do differently, identify where you're leaving money.

**Primary message hooks (use for openers):**
1. "Compare your app to top 10% in your category"
2. "See exactly what's working for apps like yours in 2026"
3. "Find your biggest revenue gap in 2 minutes"
4. "Filter by category + region + revenue tier = actionable benchmarks"

**CTAs by tactic:**
- Contest: "What % of top-performing apps offer annual plans? Drop your guess. Closest wins swag + early access. 48hr deadline."
- Comment gate: "Comment 'SOIS' below and I'll DM you the link"
- Price Radar: "Pricing Index explains the market. Price Radar shows your position inside it. Compare your pricing to apps in your exact sub-niche, country by country." CTA: Free tool link
- Thread/carousel: "Full breakdown in SOIS: [link]"
- Educational: "3 pricing mistakes that cost you 40% of revenue", "How to choose trial length using SOIS benchmarks", "5-step framework to audit your paywall strategy"
- Category deep dive: "What top 1% of [category] apps do differently" — 3–4 specific tactics backed by data
